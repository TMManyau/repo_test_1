Lemondade recipe
 1 liter water
3 lemons
3 tbsp sugar
Desolve the sugar in water until completely mixed. Squeeze all the lemons on top of your sweet water. Blend again. Crush your ice cubes in ice crusher of blender until smooth. Fill your glass with crushed ice to the half mark. Pour in your ready lemonade on the ice all the way to the top of the glass. Use a straw amd enjoy your lemonade.
